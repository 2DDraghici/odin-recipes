SARMALE

Descriere

Sarma, commonly marketed as stuffed grape leaves, is a stuffed dish in Southeastern European and Middle Eastern cuisine that comprises fermented leaves—such as cabbage, patencia dock, collard, grapevine, kale or chard leaves—rolled around a filling of grains (such as rice), minced meat, or both. Sarma is part of the broader category of stuffed dishes known as dolma.

Ingrediente

  • Foi de vita sau de varza murata
  • Carne tocata
  • Orez
  • Sare
  • Piper
  • Ou
  • Suc de rosii
  • Ceapa
  • Cimbru

Preparare

Se amesteca un kilogram de carne tocata cu ceapa sare si orez apoi amestecul se infasoara cu foitele. Se fierb intr-o oala , de preferat de lut ars , in apa cu suc de rosii si un pahar de vin.

Se tine la cuptor timp de 90 de minute la temperatura de 200 de grade celsius
